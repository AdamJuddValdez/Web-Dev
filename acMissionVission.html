Aldersgate College

Vision

A 21st Century University transforming a global network of learners for enlightenment, leadership and human service.

Mission

Aldersgate College was established to develop the power of reason, to liberate people from ignorance, penury and hopelessness,
And to empower them to commence their own journey for social and economic transformation and spiritual salvation.

Core Values

Academic Excellence.

Train students to became academically and professionally competent in the field of Arts and Letters; and physical sciences; Business; Management And Economics; Engineering and Technology; Education; And the Health Scientist.

Social Relevance.

Impress norms, values and behaviors that are socially relevant to the human development needs of the majority and the less fortunate and at the same time responsive to the ideals of a democratic society.

Spiritual Nurturance.

Evolve and inculcate a spiritual knowingness founded on an absolute Biblical truth and concurrently promoting unity, moral uprightness and dedication to Christian Service.

EOMS POLICY.

We, Aldersgate College Inc., are committed to provide high quality educational programs, service and products, and management of intellectual property;

We commit establish, review and maintain an educational orgranization policy that is supportive of its mission and vision, and for setting organizational objectives, as appropriate to the purpose and context of a UMC educational institution;

We commit to focus on the specific interaction between our educational organization, the learners, & customers, considering the needs and expectations of relevant interested parties; and to satisfy the organization's social responsibility

We commit to demonstrate our ability to consistently provide, share and facilitate the construction of knowledge, while conforming with applicable statutory and regulatory requirements, and;

We commit to the enhance the satisfaction of learners, other customers and personnel through the effective application and continual improvement of our EOMS, taking into account relevant educational, scientific and technical developments including processes for improvement of the system.

We Shall have available and maintain an educational organization policy which is communicated, understood and applied within the organization, and and which may be made available to relevant interested parties, as appropriate.
